About ReadWrite

A social reading platform called ReadWrite helps readers and writers connect online. It encourages users to write and publish original works in a variety of genres, including teen fiction, poetry, action, and adventure. ReadWrite stories were developed by local users who may later publish them for wider audiences. They range from those in the public domain from Project Gutenberg and were created in the Philippines in 2023 by MB Team. As a result, teenagers and young adults can write knowing that their work may be read by many people. It's crucial that they comprehend how to keep secure on ReadWrite as a result.

Children can readily access some categories that are appropriate for their age range and others that are not. Different forms of content are included in each of the categories, which vary from romance and fan fiction to LGBTQ+ and adventures. On ReadWrite, there are a number of settings that may be used to control what young kids see and keep them safe.
